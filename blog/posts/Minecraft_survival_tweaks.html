Minecraft survival tweaks

Recommendations

Launcher

The MultiMC launcher is highly recommended for comprehensive management of Minecraft instances.

A Microsoft account with a premium Minecraft license is required; an unofficial variant for non‑premium users is available at this repository.

Client

The Fabric mod loader (Fabric) will be used. In MultiMC, installation is performed via "Edit Instance" and "Install Fabric", then selecting the desired version.

Mods

The Fabric API is required by many Fabric mods.

Keybinds

  • CTRL + q: drop entire stack (instead of single item).

  • SHIFT + CLICK x 2: move all matching items between inventory and container.

  • RIGHT CLICK on stack: split stack in half, leaving one half and picking up the other.

  • SPACE after trading: reset villager trade interface.
